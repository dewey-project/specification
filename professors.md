# Professors

Provide information on the professors at a specific school, including general information, reviews, and courses
that they teach. 

## Fields 

|Name                     |Type              |Required?    |
|-------------------------|------------------|-------------|
|id                       |Integer           |true         |
|first_name               |String            |true         |
|last_name                |String            |true         |
